1.0 Description

This diagnostics collector applies to the following issue:

OBE-12776: Customer is not able to connect from optimize to his broker. 

It will trace creation of jms topics and queues in Broker.

OPT_10.15_Fix1 

3.0 Cautions and Warnings

5.0 Uninstallation

Note: These instructions can only be used to uninstall the 
most recently installed test patch. This action will revert

test patch.
